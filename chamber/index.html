Welcome to the Cieneguilla Chamber of Commerce in Lima, Peru

Founded in 1994, the Cieneguilla Chamber of Commerce is an organization dedicated to promoting economic and business development in the district of Cieneguilla, Lima. Our goal is to support local entrepreneurs and professionals in enhancing their businesses and contributing to the sustainable growth of our community.
